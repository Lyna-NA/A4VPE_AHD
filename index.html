What We Do Are You Ready to Finally Change Your Body

AVPE builds bridges between American and Palestinian enterprises, providing each with the knowledge, contacts, and context they need to collaborate and thrive

We can help Americans and Palestinians with requests for information, advice, coaching mentoring, training and other ways that solve problems, transfer knowledge, build organizational capabilities and broaden perspectives.
